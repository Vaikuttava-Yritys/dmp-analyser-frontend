Privacy

How We Handle Your Data

The ReproAI Analyser processes the text you submit to provide analysis results and improve the tool. Your data is stored securely within the European Economic Area (EEA) and may be retained for up to 60 days to allow access to your analysis results. You can request immediate deletion at any time.

Third-Party AI Services

We use third-party AI services (OpenAI, Anthropic/Claude) for text analysis. These services may retain data for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring before permanent deletion. We do not use these services in ways that contribute to public model training databases.

Security Measures

Your files are processed in isolated, controlled environments using enterprise-grade security practices:

  • Role-based access control
  • Encrypted data transfers and storage
  • Audit logging
  • Isolation between user sessions

GDPR Compliance

Our tool is hosted on Microsoft Azure in Europe (North Europe region). All data processing and storage remain within the EEA to comply with GDPR and institutional data protection requirements.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact the system administrator.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
